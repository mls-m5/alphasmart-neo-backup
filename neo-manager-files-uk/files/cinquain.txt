CINQUAIN – "Five Line Poem"
[ ] Line 1 - One word that identified the subject of the poem.
[ ] Line 2 - Two words to describe the subject.
[ ] Line 3 - Three words to describe an action of the subject. This means what the subject does or what is done to the subject.
[ ] Line 4 - Four words that express an emotional response toward the subject.
[ ] Line 5 - A one-word synonym of the subject.
